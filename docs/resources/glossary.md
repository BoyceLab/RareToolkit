# Glossary

## Advocacy group registry
A database or structured program where participants contribute health, genetic, and lived-experience information.

## GA4GH
Global Alliance for Genomics and Health.

## HPO
Human Phenotype Ontology, a standard vocabulary for phenotypic abnormalities.

## Ontology mapping
Connecting a patient-facing question or answer to a standard concept identifier.

## Phenopacket
A GA4GH format for exchanging structured phenotype and disease information.

## Provenance
Information about where a data point came from, such as self-report, caregiver report, clinician note, or uploaded report.
