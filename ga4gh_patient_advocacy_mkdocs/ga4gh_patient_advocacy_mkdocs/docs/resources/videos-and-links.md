## Suggested way to use these resources

For most advocacy groups, a good onboarding sequence is:

1. watch one introductory HPO video,
2. review the HPO site,
3. review the GenomeConnect sample survey,
4. read the Phenopackets overview,
5. design your own core registry fields and mapping dictionary.
