# About this site

This MkDocs site is a starter design for a rare disease patient advocacy audience.

It emphasizes:

- plain language,
- standards behind the scenes,
- HPO-backed questionnaires,
- GA4GH Phenopackets as an export target,
- practical governance and quality steps.

## Suggested next enhancements

- add your organization branding,
- embed short explainer videos,
- add downloadable questionnaire templates,
- publish your own data dictionary,
- include disease-specific examples.
